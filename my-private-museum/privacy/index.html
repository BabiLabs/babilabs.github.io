Privacy policy

Private by design, not by promise alone.

My Private Museum works offline. The publisher does not receive your collections, object details, photos, exports, or backups.

Last updated: 18 July 2026

1. Who is responsible?

My Private Museum is published by Ali Babi. Privacy questions can be sent to ababi@gmx.de.

2. Scope of this policy

This policy covers the My Private Museum mobile application for Android and iOS and this informational website. The app and website have different technical roles, which are explained separately below.

3. Data inside the app

The app can process information that you choose to enter or import, including:

  • collection names, descriptions, styles, and covers;
  • object titles, notes, collector metadata, and dates;
  • photos selected from your library or taken with the camera;
  • app preferences such as theme and language; and
  • locally created exports and backup information.

This information is stored locally in the app's private storage on your device. It is not uploaded to Ali Babi, an application server, an analytics platform, or an advertising network. The app has no account system and no cloud synchronization.

4. Photos and camera

Photo-library and camera access is used only when you ask to add a photo. Your operating system controls the relevant picker or permission prompt. Imported images are copied into private app-managed storage. The original source photo is not altered.

5. Sharing

Sharing starts only after an explicit action. The app renders a new share image from the photo and details you selected, then hands that file to the operating system's share sheet. The receiving app or service is selected by you and operates under its own privacy policy. Ali Babi does not receive the shared file.

6. HTML gallery export

HTML galleries are generated locally as static files. They contain only the collection content and options you selected. After saving an export, you control where it is kept, copied, uploaded, or published. Publishing an export can make its contents publicly accessible; the app does not publish it for you.

7. Backup and restore

Backups are created locally and saved only where you choose. You may create an encrypted backup protected by a password or consciously choose an unencrypted backup. The password is never sent to Ali Babi and cannot be recovered by the publisher. Anyone who obtains an unencrypted backup may be able to read its contents.

8. Storage, retention, and deletion

App-managed information remains on the device until you edit or delete it. Deleting an object or collection removes its managed records and associated managed media. Removing the app may delete its private database and managed photos. Files you exported, shared, or backed up outside the app remain under your control and must be deleted separately.

9. Analytics, advertising, and tracking

The app contains no advertising, analytics, user profiling, tracking SDK, social login, or developer-operated network service. It does not sell personal information or share it for targeted advertising.

10. Website hosting

This static website is hosted using GitHub Pages. We do not add analytics, advertising, tracking scripts, contact forms, or non-essential cookies. GitHub may process technical request information, such as IP address, browser information, and timestamps, to deliver and protect its service. Ali Babi does not receive GitHub's server logs. More information is available in the GitHub General Privacy Statement.

11. Children

My Private Museum is a general collection-management tool and is not directed at children under 13. The publisher does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

12. International use

The app can be used in different countries while its collection information remains on the user's device. If you independently share, export, or publish information across borders, you choose the destination and are responsible for that action.

13. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the app, website, or applicable requirements change. The current version and update date will remain available on this page.

14. Contact

For privacy questions, contact Ali Babi at ababi@gmx.de.
