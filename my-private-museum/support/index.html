Common questions

Can you see or recover my collection?

No. The app has no account or cloud service, and the publisher does not receive your collection. Create your own backup before changing devices or uninstalling the app.

Can you recover my backup password?

No. Backup passwords never leave your device. A lost password cannot be recovered by the publisher.

Why did my photos disappear after uninstalling?

Managed photos are stored inside the app's private storage and may be deleted when the app is removed. Files previously exported or backed up outside the app are separate.

Why does sharing depend on other apps?

My Private Museum hands a newly rendered image to the system share sheet. Available destinations depend on the compatible applications installed on your device.

What should a useful bug report include?

Please include your device model, Android or iOS version, the action you attempted, and what happened. Do not send private collection photos or backup passwords unless they are genuinely necessary and you consciously choose to do so.
